# Greenhouse Generator

Developers: Runying Chen, Haochen Tong, Aditi Kumar

Grow your own cozy greenhouse empire. Plant tiny seeds, watch them sprout into happy little crops, and earn coins as they flourish. Expand your row of pots, boost your growth with upgrades, and unlock fertilizer to supercharge your garden. Tap, plant, harvest, and relax as your peaceful VR greenhouse comes to life 🌱
